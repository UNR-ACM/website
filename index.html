Introduction

ACM is a club that is all about Computer Science and Engineering at UNR. We are all CSE majors trying to learn what we can about programming, hardware, and anything about computers. We have monthly meetings and events to help network with local tech companies and hold workshops to help students learn about topics not well covered in curriculum.
